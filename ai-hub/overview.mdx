AI Hub is DemoKraft AI's content activation layer — the bridge between the demos you create in AI Studio and the revenue conversations your sales team needs to have. Instead of sending prospects a passive video link that plays and disappears, AI Hub deploys your content as a 24/7 live qualification experience: an AI Demo Agent greets every visitor, learns what they care about, routes them through the content that matters most, and hands your team a complete buyer profile before anyone picks up the phone.

## How it works

The content you build in AI Studio flows directly into AI Hub — no manual uploading, no rebuilding. Click **Send to Hub** from AI Studio's export panel and your demo is live in your Hub content library, ready to be assembled into sessions and shared with prospects.

Once live, every session runs the same way:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Content arrives from AI Studio">
    Videos, step-by-step docs, and GIFs created in AI Studio appear automatically in your Hub content library. Updates made in AI Studio sync to Hub with a single click — your deployed demos stay current without rebuilding from scratch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Demo Agent greets the visitor">
    When a prospect opens your session link, the AI Demo Agent sends a personalised welcome message and introduces itself. The agent's name, avatar, and opening message are all configurable to match your brand voice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent asks qualifying questions">
    The agent asks up to five qualifying questions — role, team size, buying timeline, key challenges, and more. Answers can be multiple-choice or free-text. This takes the visitor under 60 seconds and determines exactly which content they see next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Content routes to match their answers">
    Based on the visitor's answers, the agent navigates them through the content path most relevant to their use case. A Sales leader sees a different sequence than a Developer or a Customer Success Manager — even though they all opened the same session link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visitor asks questions in real time">
    Throughout the session, the AI agent is available in a chat panel. Visitors can ask anything — integration questions, pricing questions, technical questions — and the agent draws on your product knowledge base to respond accurately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete buyer profile delivered">
    When the session ends, your sales team receives a structured Lead Intelligence profile in AI Hub, their CRM, and Slack: qualifying answers, content watched, questions asked, time spent, and a 0–100 intent score. The first call is never cold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Plan | Price | Sessions included |
|---|---|---|
| Hub Starter | $50/month | 50 sessions |
| Hub Growth | $150/month | 150 sessions |
| Hub Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

<Note>
  Static Playlist and Conversational Playlist views are **not** counted as sessions — only AI Demo Agent Guided Sessions consume session credits. You can run unlimited playlists alongside your Guided Sessions without affecting your allowance.
</Note>
