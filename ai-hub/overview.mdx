Creating the content is only half the battle. Demokraft AI Hub is the hosting, sharing, and analytics engine that turns that video into a 24/7 working asset. The Hub moves beyond **passive viewing** to **active engagement**. 

Demokraft AI Hub enables you to share your videos as intelligent playlists. You can create and configure playlists as per different needs and finally share those playlist links. Playlist links can be shared in outreach emails, social media posts, docs, or directly integrated with your website.

---

## Instant Gratification & Personalization

Buyers and viewers today want self-guided exploration. Demokraft enables:

* **Instant Access:** No scheduling required. Prospects and viewers engage with 24/7 AI-guided, conversational, or static playlists immediately.
* **Personalized Guided Flows:** The AI adapts the demo flow based on buyer or viewer intent, ensuring they see exactly what matters to them.
* **Seamless Integration:** The Hub integrates with your tools (Slack and email are currently supported, with CRM integrations coming soon), pushing engagement data directly to your tech stack.

## Versatile Sharing Modes

Demokraft gives you the flexibility to present your content in the format best suited to your audience's intent:

* **Static Playlist:** Share a classic experience (similar to a YouTube playlist) for educational or "how-to" sequences where the viewer simply wants to watch and learn.
* **Conversational Playlist:** Transform passive viewing into a conversational experience where viewers can not just see the videos they want, but even ask questions and get answers from an AI Agent.
* **AI Demo Agent Guided Session:** The most advanced mode for sales. An AI agent actively guides the viewer, qualifying them in real-time and recommending appropriate videos based on their needs.

## Uncompromised Brand Integrity

Demokraft AI Hub prioritizes your corporate identity, ensuring a premium environment that aligns strictly with your business goals:

* **Your Domain, Your Brand:** Host videos, docs, and playlists integrated with your website or on your own custom domain. This builds trust with prospects.
* **Immersive Professional Experience:** Deliver a polished, corporate-grade viewing environment where the viewer’s attention is dedicated solely to your content and value proposition.
* **Strategic Conversion Paths:** Maintain full control over the buyer journey. Once a session concludes, automatically guide prospects to your defined Call-to-Action (CTA).

## Enterprise-Grade Access Control

In B2B, not all content is meant for the public eye. Demokraft AI Hub offers granular control over who accesses your playlists:

1. **Open Access:** Best for top-of-funnel marketing or open sharing of content.
2. **Credential-Based Access:** User credentials like name and email can be requested before viewing, but are not verified.
3. **Verified Access & Whitelisting:** Allow access only to verified credentials. In addition, you can restrict access to specific email addresses or entire corporate domains (e.g., `@client-company.com`). This ensures confidential pricing or roadmaps are only viewable by authorized identities.

## Analytics and Insights

Analytics and insights are the key final outcomes of the content engagement. Actionable insights is what makes hub valuable.
Insights include:
* Number of Views.
* Per session level details like viewer details, videos watched, conversation details, lead score (if applicable).
* Playlist level stats.
