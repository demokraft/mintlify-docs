AI Hub gives you three distinct ways to deploy your demo content — each designed for a different audience, goal, and level of interactivity. Picking the right mode is the single fastest way to improve how your content performs. A Static Playlist is perfect for onboarding a new customer cohort; that same content delivered as an AI Demo Agent Guided Session would feel intrusive and unnecessary. Choose the mode that fits the context, not the one that sounds most impressive.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Static Playlist">
    ## Static Playlist

    A Static Playlist delivers your content as a structured, sequential library — videos and docs that viewers watch at their own pace with no AI interaction. Think of it as a professional, branded version of a YouTube playlist, fully tracked and under your control.

    **What it is:** content items play in the order you set them. Viewers can watch from start to finish or jump to any item directly. There is no AI agent, no qualifying questions, and no chat panel. What you get is a clean, distraction-free viewing experience backed by meaningful completion analytics.

    **Best for:**
    - New customer onboarding flows
    - Internal team training and L&D modules
    - Product knowledge bases
    - Event follow-up content sent post-conference

    **How to create a Static Playlist:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create the playlist">
        In AI Hub, click **New Session → Static Playlist**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add your content">
        Add content items from your Hub library in the order you want viewers to see them. Drag to reorder.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set access control">
        Choose your access tier — Open, Credential-Based, or Verified/Whitelisted — to match the sensitivity of the content. See [Access Control](/ai-hub/access-control) for details.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish and share">
        Click **Publish**, copy the shareable link, and distribute it to your audience.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Analytics you get:**
    - Completion rates per content item
    - Time spent per item
    - Drop-off points showing exactly where viewers stop watching
    - Total viewer count and unique viewer count
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Conversational Playlist">
    ## Conversational Playlist

    A Conversational Playlist adds an AI assistant layer on top of your standard playlist experience. Viewers watch your content at their own pace and can ask the AI agent questions at any point — the agent draws on your product knowledge base to answer accurately in real time.

    **What it is:** your content plays in sequence as it would in a Static Playlist, but a chat panel is always visible. Viewers can type or speak questions whenever something sparks their curiosity. The AI answers from the knowledge base you configure — your Hub videos, docs, GIFs, and any custom FAQ entries you add. Questions and answers are logged and surfaced in the lead intelligence report.

    **Best for:**
    - Top-of-funnel website demos where buyers want to explore at their own pace
    - Partner and reseller enablement portals
    - Investor demos and analyst briefings
    - Pre-sales evaluation content where buyers have specific technical questions

    **How to create a Conversational Playlist:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create the playlist">
        In AI Hub, click **New Session → Conversational Playlist**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add your content">
        Add content items from your Hub library in the order you want viewers to see them.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure the knowledge base scope">
        In **Agent Settings → Knowledge Base**, toggle on the content items the AI agent should draw on when answering viewer questions. Add Custom FAQ entries for pricing, security, and common objections.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set access control and publish">
        Choose your access tier, publish, and share the link.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Analytics adds (on top of Static Playlist):**
    - Full question logs — every question a viewer asked and every answer the agent gave
    - Most-asked topics across all viewers — reveals gaps in your demo content
    - Pause-to-question moments — the exact timestamps where viewers stopped watching to ask something, indicating high-interest sections
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="AI Demo Agent Guided Session">
    ## AI Demo Agent Guided Session

    The most advanced and highest-converting mode. The AI Demo Agent takes an active role from the first moment — greeting your prospect, asking qualifying questions, routing them to the right content based on their answers, scoring their intent, and providing in-session Q&A throughout. This is full AI-guided lead qualification at scale.

    **What it is:** the AI Demo Agent greets visitors with a personalised welcome message, then asks up to five qualifying questions before any content plays. Based on their answers, the agent routes each visitor to the content path most relevant to their role, team size, or use case. Every visitor who opens the same link can see an entirely different demo sequence. The agent stays active throughout, answering questions and scoring engagement signals. At the end, it delivers a closing CTA message configured by you.

    **Best for:**
    - Outbound sales prospecting and qualification (replace or supplement the intro demo call)
    - ABM campaigns targeting named accounts
    - Presales technical evaluation sessions
    - Conference and event demo kiosks

    **How to create an AI Demo Agent Guided Session:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create the session">
        In AI Hub, click **New Session → AI Demo Agent Guided Session**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure the agent persona">
        Set the agent's display name, introductory message, and avatar image. See [AI Demo Agent](/ai-hub/ai-demo-agent) for full configuration options.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add qualifying questions and routing rules">
        Add up to five qualifying questions and map each answer to a specific content path. Configure fallback behaviour for visitors whose answers don't match any routing rule.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect CRM sync">
        Connect HubSpot or Salesforce so Lead Intelligence profiles push to your CRM automatically when sessions complete. See [Lead Intelligence](/ai-hub/lead-intelligence).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish and share or embed">
        Publish the session, copy the shareable link, or embed it on your website or landing page using the embed code from the **Share** menu.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    **Analytics adds (on top of Conversational Playlist):**
    - Qualification scores from 0–100 per visitor
    - Full CRM Lead Intelligence profiles with visitor identity, engagement depth, and intent signals
    - Routing paths taken — see which content paths visitors followed
    - Intent signal timelines — a chronological log of every engagement action
    - Meeting bookings attributed to Hub sessions
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Use case quick reference

Use this table to choose a mode at a glance. When in doubt, the AI Demo Agent Guided Session delivers the most data — but only use it when it fits the context naturally.

| Use case | Recommended mode |
|---|---|
| Onboarding new customers | Static Playlist |
| Internal team training | Static Playlist |
| Top-of-funnel website demo | Conversational Playlist |
| Partner and reseller enablement | Conversational Playlist |
| Outbound sales prospecting | AI Demo Agent Guided Session |
| ABM target account campaign | AI Demo Agent Guided Session |
| Event kiosk or conference demo | AI Demo Agent Guided Session |

<Note>
  Static Playlist and Conversational Playlist views do **not** consume AI Hub session credits. Only AI Demo Agent Guided Sessions count against your monthly session allowance. See [AI Hub pricing](/ai-hub/overview#ai-hub-pricing) for credit volumes and plan options.
</Note>

<Tip>
  You can have unlimited active playlists and sessions running simultaneously under a single workspace. There is no cap on how many you create or publish — only Guided Sessions consume credits.
</Tip>
