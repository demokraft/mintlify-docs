Playlists enable organized delivery of video content tailored for various business use cases. This guide takes you through the step-by-step process of setting up and distributing playlists.

## Accessing the Playlist Area

1. Navigate to the **Hub** section in your dashboard.
2. Go to the **Library** and select the **Playlist** area. Here, you can view existing playlists or manage new ones.
3. To initiate a new playlist, click **Add Playlist**.

### Access Options
Demokraft offers multiple access control options to regulate playlist availability:

<Frame>
![Access Options](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_2.jpg)

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Open Access" icon="unlock">
    Allows any user to access the playlist without requiring credentials, facilitating broad availability
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credentials-Based Access" icon="id-card">
    Prompts users to submit information such as an email ID to gain access, enabling basic user identification.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Verification with Credentials" icon="shield-check">
    Submitted user details undergo mandatory verification using OTP before access is granted, ensuring maximum security
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Email and Domain Access Controls

In verification with credentials based access type, you can restrict access further by specifying allowed email addresses or specific domains. This precise control prevents unauthorized viewing and ensures your videos target the intended audience.

![Email Restrictions](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_6.jpg)

Demokraft also allows you to filter access strictly by email type—such as permitting only personal domain extensions or business domain extensions.

![Personal vs Business](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_7.jpg)

  <Step title="Enhance Agent Context">
    Provide supplementary details like a welcome message, company name, product name, and a rich product description. This enriches the agent’s context to improve automated answers.
    ![Agent Context](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_12.jpg)

  <Step title="Define Target Audience">
    Specify who the playlist's target audience is to ensure context and recommendation logic match customer demographics perfectly.
    ![Audience](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_13.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Layout Orientation">
    Select your display format: horizontal or vertical orientation. You can also position video queues to the right side with vertical layout, mirroring a YouTube-style interface for familiar customer navigation.

  <Step title="Add Custom Agent Instructions">

    Click **Save and Continue** to advance.

## Organizing Videos into Categories

Organize your videos logically into distinct categories for user navigation.

![Organizing Categories](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_17.jpg)

* **Creating Custom Categories:** Add a category by entering a category name and clicking **Add Category**. Newly added categories will quickly surface with available videos ready for assignment.
  ![Custom Categories](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_17.jpg)
* **Managing Assignments:** Easily remove videos from a category to place them back into your unassigned list, or swap them across other groups.
  ![Assignments](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_18.jpg)
* **Sequence Control:** Preview playlists and sequential structures. You can map out multi-category flows to customize the end-user viewing track.
  ![Playlist Overview](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_19.jpg)

When users start a live session, the structured categories are presented seamlessly for simple topic filtering.

![User Access View](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_20.jpg)

Click **Save and Continue** to finalize your setup.

![Finalize Changes](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/6687be00-2721-4896-ba91-e1209228f1bd/video_screenshot_file/frame_21.jpg)
