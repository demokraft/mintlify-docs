---
title: "Control Who Can Access Your AI Hub Playlist Sessions and Content"
sidebarTitle: Playlist Access Control
description: Set exactly who can access your Hub Playlist, from open public links to email-verified whitelisted access for confidential or enterprise content.
---

Every piece of content you deploy through AI Hub does not need the same level of protection — a public-facing product demo and a confidential roadmap preview have very different audiences, and your access settings should reflect that. AI Hub gives you three access tiers that range from a fully open link anyone can click to email-verified, domain-whitelisted access you control down to the individual email address. You set the access level per per playlist, so each piece of content gets exactly the right amount of protection.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Open Access">
    ## Open Access

    Anyone with the link can view your content. No login, no password, no verification required. This is the fastest way to get content in front of a broad audience.

    **Best for:**
    - Public-facing product demos on your website or landing pages
    - Top-of-funnel outreach links sent via email or LinkedIn
    - Conference and event demo kiosk screens

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Credential-Based Access">
    ## Credential-Based Access

    Viewers must provide credentials before they can see any content. But the credentials are not verified.

    **Best for:**
    - Draft demos shared with specific prospects before a call
    - Partner portals with tiered content access
    - Beta or pre-launch content shared with selected accounts

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Verified / Whitelisted Access">
    ## Verified / Whitelisted Access

    Viewers verify their identity via a one-time email passcode (OTP) before accessing any content.
    You control who qualifies by whitelisting specific email addresses or entire email domains. No password required — verification is instant for anyone on your whitelist.

    **Best for:**
    - Confidential product roadmaps shared with named enterprise accounts
    - Board-level and investor content (whitelist specific personal emails)
    - Lead qualification with verified creds.
    - Trainees/employees with verified email ids.
    - Sensitive internal content (whitelist your company domain)
    - High-value named account content for ABM campaigns

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

All three access tiers work fully with the different sharing modes. Verified / Whitelisted access provides the richest lead data because visitor identity is confirmed at the point of entry — every engagement signal in the session is attributed to a named, verified individual.
