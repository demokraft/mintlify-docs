The **Insights** section offers comprehensive dashboards and analytics that facilitate close monitoring of your content performance, user engagement, and lead qualification. These tools support data-driven management of your Studio and Hub assets and get actionable insights.

## Accessing the Insights Section

After logging in to the **Demokraft AI Hub**, locate the **Insights** section in the left navigation panel. Click on it to open the primary Insights dashboard, which contains several subsections presenting various analytics and performance metrics.

## Analytics Overview

### Key Statistics
The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of key statistics across multiple platform components including your **Studio**, and **Hub**. It covers different playlist types (such as guided, conversational, and static playlists) as well as varying use-case and access-type categories to provide comprehensive usage data.

![Key Statistics](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_2.jpg)

### Comprehensive & Centralized View
This section consolidates data from both the **Studio** and **Hub** platforms into a single interface. This unified view eliminates the need to switch between multiple screens or modules, streamlining your ability to track activity metrics and assess content impact efficiently.

## Lead Qualification Dashboard

The lead dashboard isolates analytics for **lead qualification playlists** specifically, offering deep-dive tools to track engagement and evaluate leads generated through your video content.
This dashboard is mainly applicable to Guided conversational playlists.

![Lead Qualification Dashboard](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_8.jpg)

### Core Engagement Metrics
The interface captures crucial data parameters for lead management:

*   **Traffic:** Unique and repeat visitors.
*   **Consumption:** Total videos watched, total demo time, and average demo duration.
*   **Completion:** Overall demo completion rates.
*   **Lead Scoring:** Automated evaluation value calculated for each session.

![Lead Engagement Metrics and Scoring](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_8.jpg)

<Card title="Understanding Lead Scoring (1 to 5)" icon="star">
  Lead scoring assigns a value from **1 to 5** for each session based on the conversational data, detected buying intent, and alignment with the prospect's needs . 
  
  *A score of **5** indicates a highly qualified lead.*
</Card>

## Session Reviews & Interaction Analysis

### In-Depth Session Lists
Review a detailed leads and viewers list that breaks down every individual session . This allows for an in-depth analysis of user behaviors and actions during their interactions .

![Leads and Viewers Session Review](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_10.jpg)

For any specific user session, you can analyze:
*   Exactly what videos the lead watched and their total duration of consumption .
*   Which videos attracted the highest level of interest .
*   User-submitted feedback or ratings, allowing for better content optimization and targeted follow-up .

![Session Content and Feedback Analysis](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_11.jpg)

### Score Justification
Lead qualification details display the assigned lead score accompanied by a plain-text rationale . This justification derives directly from the buying intent detected within the conversational logs, giving sales teams clear context for lead prioritization .

![Reviewing Lead Qualification Details](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_11.jpg)

## Conversation Trails

You can access full conversation trails that record the verbatim interaction between the user and the AI agent during a session . This feature supports the review of conversational dynamics for improved lead nurturing .
Conversational Trail is only available based on your subscription plan and conversational or guided conversational playlist type. It is not applicable to static playlist type.

![Conversation Trail Access](https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/dca5540c-3f1e-494a-950f-ef227365a8d7/studio/bbcbd23d-99b5-4684-b3aa-2af31ed68d64/video_screenshot_file/frame_13.jpg)
