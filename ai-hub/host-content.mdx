## Accessing Demokraft AI Hub

After logging into the Demokraft AI application, locate the **Hub** section in the navigation panel. This section provides access to manage all your content centrally by allowing you to switch between **Videos**, **Documents**, and **Playlists** categories.

## Uploading Videos

To upload a video file from your system, click **Add Video**. During the upload process, you can provide the following attributes to enhance management and searchability:

* Title
* Thumbnail image
* Description
* Access type (e.g., public or restricted)
* Tags

These details facilitate easier organization and retrieval of your videos.

## Organizing Videos into Playlists

Once a video is uploaded, it is added to your content list. From here, you can organize videos into playlists for better content structure and user navigation. This functionality operates similarly to playlist management on platforms like YouTube, allowing you to group related videos effectively.
Will cover how to manage playlists in a different guide.

## Uploading Documents for Conversational Engagement

The Hub also supports uploading documents relevant to your product to enable conversational AI engagement. Currently supported document formats include:

* PDF
* Microsoft Word (DOC/DOCX)
* Plain text (TXT)

Uploading these documents allows the AI agent to access detailed product context during user interactions.

This contextual information improves the AI's ability to answer user questions effectively during sessions, increasing the quality and relevance of interactions.

## Adding Documents and Metadata

Use the **Add Documents** button to upload a relevant file. After uploading, add a descriptive title and description to the document. This metadata assists the AI agent in processing the file properly and building an accurate product knowledge base for conversational purposes.
