This guide walks you through every step of creating a complete, AI-enhanced product demo video — from uploading your raw screen recording all the way to publishing a shareable link and activating your content as a live lead qualification experience in AI Hub. You don't need any video editing experience, and you don't need a perfect recording to start. DemoKraft AI handles the polish.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload your recording">
    Navigate to **AI Studio** in the left sidebar, then click **New Project**.

    Choose how you want to bring your content in:

    - **Upload a file** — drag and drop an `.mp4`, `.mov`, or `.webm` screen recording directly into the project window
    - **Upload a deck** — import a PowerPoint (`.pptx`) or Google Slides file and DemoKraft AI generates a narrated walkthrough from your slides
    - **Record now** — launch the in-browser recorder to capture your screen, your camera, or both simultaneously — no additional software required

    Give your project a descriptive name (for example, _Product Walkthrough — Q3 2026_) and click **Create** to begin processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and enhance the script">
    Once your file finishes processing (typically 1–3 minutes), DemoKraft AI analyses your narration or slide text and automatically generates an enhanced script that improves clarity, flow, and persuasiveness while preserving your intent.

    1. Open the **Script** panel on the right side of the editor.
    2. Read through the AI-suggested script and edit any segment by clicking it directly — changes sync to the voiceover automatically.
    3. Click **Approve Script** when you're satisfied to lock in the script and move to voiceover.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your voiceover">
    Replace the original audio — background noise, filler words, and all — with a professional AI voice:

    1. Open the **Voiceover** panel.
    2. Browse the voice library and filter by language, accent, or gender to find the right fit for your audience.
    3. Click **Preview** to hear a 15-second sample of any voice with your actual script.
    4. Click **Apply Voiceover** to synchronise the AI voice to your video timeline.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add visual effects (optional)">
    Add production-grade effects to guide your viewer's attention and bring your demo to life:

    1. Open the **Effects** panel.
    2. Click the moment in the timeline where you want an effect to appear.
    3. Choose from: **Zoom & Blur**, **Spotlight**, **Arrows**, **Image Overlay**, **Text Overlay**, **Animated Text**, **Animated Objects**, or **Motion Scenes**.
    4. Fine-tune each effect's settings in the properties panel on the right, then drag it to the correct position on the timeline.

    Effects are added as non-destructive timeline layers, so you can adjust or remove them at any time without re-processing your video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your content">
    A single DemoKraft AI project generates three assets simultaneously — you don't have to choose:

    | Asset | Format | Best for |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | Video | MP4, up to 4K | Demo calls, website embeds, social media |
    | Step-by-step doc | Web page / PDF | Help centres, onboarding, sales follow-up |
    | GIF | Animated GIF | Email signatures, Slack messages, product teasers |

    Click **Export** in the top toolbar, select the formats you want, then download the files locally or copy the sharing link for each asset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate in AI Hub (optional)">
    Turn your finished video into an AI-guided interactive demo that qualifies leads or allows users to access your content, before you even speak with them:

    1. Click **Hub** from the side bar on app home screen, select **Videos** and upload your product videos.
    2. Click **Documents** tab and upload your product documents for AI Agent to use to answer product questions.
    3. Click **Playlists** and create playlists with videos and documents you need.
    4. In AI Hub, choose a Playlist **Type**:
       - **Static Playlist** — a curated, self-navigated playlist of your videos
       - **Conversational Playlist** — an AI that recommends videos based on visitor responses
       - **AI Demo Agent Lead Qualification **— a fully conversational AI that greets visitors, guides them through the demo, and qualifies their intent in real time
    5. Configure **Access type** as per your needs. 
    6. Save the playlist and once playlist list,  copy your playlist link or embed code and share it with prospects, add it to your website, or drop it into your CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  During your free trial, focus on creating one complete demo video and sharing it with a real prospect. Seeing the lead intelligence features — engagement tracking, CRM sync, and AI-qualified intent signals — in a live scenario is the fastest way to understand DemoKraft AI's revenue impact.
</Tip>
