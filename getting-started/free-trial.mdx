When you sign up for DemoKraft AI, you get immediate access to a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. The trial gives you hands-on access to the core features of AI Studio and AI Hub so you can create real product content, share it with real prospects or users, and see the insights data flow. This page explains exactly what's included, how to make the most of your 14 days, and what happens when the trial ends.

## What's included in your free trial

Your trial includes everything you need to create and share a complete AI-enhanced demo:

- **14 days** of access — no credit card required
- Up to **3 projects** (videos, docs, and GIFs combined)
- **10-minute** maximum input recording length
- **1 seat**
- **720p** export resolution
- **AI voiceover** — full access to the voice library
- **AI script enhancement** — automatic script improvement on every upload
- **Language translation** — available in 2 languages
- **Visual effects** — zoom, spotlight, overlays, animated text, and more
- **CRM integrations** — connect HubSpot or Salesforce
- **AI Hub access** — limited AI Demo Agent sessions for evaluation
- **Watermark** on all exports (automatically removed when you upgrade to any paid plan)

<Tip>
  Use a real product screen recording — not a test clip — from day one. The AI script enhancement and voiceover are most impressive when they're working with your actual product narrative.
</Tip>

## Subscription plans

There is a separate subscription for Studio for content creation and separate subscription for Hub to host, share and analyse engagement. Each have separate subscription plans. You can subscribe either or both of them as needed. You can find the different available plans in **Settings → Billing** section. Or you could visit our website [pricing page](https://demokraft.ai/plans-and-pricing/).

## Upgrade anytime

You can upgrade to a paid plan at any point during or after your trial — you don't need to wait for it to expire. To upgrade:

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**.
3. Choose **Starter**, **Growth**, or contact us for an **Enterprise** quote.
4. Enter your payment details — your account upgrades immediately.

All 3 of your trial projects are preserved when you upgrade. Watermarks are removed from all previously exported assets automatically.

<Warning>
  If your trial expires without upgrading, you cannot create new projects or export content. Your existing projects and assets are retained for 30 days, then permanently deleted. If you upgrade within that 30-day window, all your content is restored immediately. Don't wait — upgrade before your trial ends to avoid any interruption to your work.
</Warning>
