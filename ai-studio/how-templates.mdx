## Understanding Templates

Templates accelerate video production by providing preassembled scenes and video structures that streamline your workflow.
Create professional product videos with motion graphics in minutes.


## Components of Templates

Templates combine predesigned scenes, layouts, backgrounds, objects, and effects into a single starting point, allowing you to create videos more efficiently.


## Types of Templates

There are two main types of templates:

* **Scene templates:** Individual scenes that you can add to existing videos.
* **Video templates:** Complete video structures composed of multiple scenes tailored to specific video types.

## Scene Templates Overview

Scene templates provide a full scene with all essential components ready for integration into your current video projects.

## Video Templates Overview

Video templates offer a predefined set of scenes assembled into a complete video, centered on the style and purpose of the content you intend to create.
