## Overview of Scene Options

The system provides scene options that can be applied to your project. These options include Intro Template, Outro Template, and Chapter Break Template.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/image-24-1024x513.png" alt="Overview of Scene Options" />
</Frame>

## Accessing Scene Options in Studio Mode

Open your existing project in studio mode to access the scene options. The scenes section is located on the left-hand side of the interface.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/2d1690e8-a425-4baf-82d4-7c59b8aca885/studio/d702b0dc-1251-4fba-ade5-11868fd17295/video_screenshot_file/frame_2.jpg" alt="Scene Template Options UI" />
</Frame>

## Available Scene Types

There are three primary scene template options:

1. Intro Template
2. Outro Template
3. Chapter Break Template You select one of these to customize and add to your project.

  <img src="https://storage.googleapis.com/dk-transcoder-assets/seller/2d1690e8-a425-4baf-82d4-7c59b8aca885/studio/d702b0dc-1251-4fba-ade5-11868fd17295/video_screenshot_file/frame_3.jpg" alt="Selecting an Intro Template" />

## Applying an Intro Template

To apply an intro template, click on the Intro Template option. Then choose your preferred template from the available options by clicking the add button next to it.

## Applying the Intro Template

Click the Apply button to generate and add the intro segment to the beginning of your project. This action finalizes the intro's inclusion.

## Accessing and Applying Chapter Breaks

Navigate back to the scenes section to select the Chapter Break option. You can apply chapter breaks at any point within your project timeline to segment content. Place the player marker on a scene after which you want to add the Chapter break. Add the Chapter break template from options. Scene will get added to your video.
