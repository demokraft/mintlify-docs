## Overview of Download Process

This guide explains the steps to download your finalized video and document files, ensuring you can easily preserve and share your content.

## Initiating Video Download

Once you have edited and finalized your video or document, you can begin the export process. In the Studio editor with video project open, start by selecting  click **Export** followed by **Download** to proceed.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-185541-1024x575.png" alt="Initiating Video Export" />
</Frame>

## Selecting Video Format

You will see two format options for your video: **MP4** and **GIF**. If you choose GIF, select the size you prefer, then click **Export**. The system will prepare the file in the background. When ready, a download link will appear for you to save the file to your device.
If you select mp4 format, you can choose the resolution config available based on your subscription plan. We support 720p, 1080p, 2k, 4k options along with different fps rates.

</Frame>

## Downloading Documents Made Simple

To download your document, click on it first. After completing your edits, select **Export** and then choose **Download** to access format options for saving your file.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_4-5-1024x491.jpg" alt="Document Export Options" />
</Frame>

## Document Format Choices

Your document can be downloaded in two formats: 

* **Markdown:** Best for local management or editing in any Markdown-compatible environment.
* **HTML:** Suitable for direct browser viewing or web embedding purposes.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_4-5-1024x491.jpg" alt="Markdown or HTML Format Selection" />
</Frame>

## Using and Saving HTML Documents

Selecting the HTML format provides a file that opens in your web browser or can be embedded directly into web pages. After selecting your preferred format, click **Download** to save the file onto your computer.

  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_5-4-1024x491.jpg" alt="Final Document Download" />
</Frame>

## Closing Remarks

By following these straightforward download steps, you can efficiently save and utilize your videos and documents in formats that best suit your needs.
