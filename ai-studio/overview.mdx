## Studio is the flagship creation experience within Demokraft AI.

Purpose-built for software products, it enables anyone to create polished product content without requiring advanced video production or design expertise.

Whether starting from a screen recording, creating graphical product videos with motion graphics from scratch, customizing professionally designed templates, or soon generating complete product videos from a simple prompt, Studio dramatically reduces production effort while maintaining professional quality.

Studio includes:

- Visual Editor
- Screen Recording
- Presentation to video
- Motion Graphics
- Templates
- Reusable Objects with Device Mockups
- Branding Tools
- Animations & Visual Effects
- AI Voice Generation
- AI Script enhance
- Multilingual Screen Recording (14 Languages)
- Translation & Localization (39 Languages)
- Prompt-to-Product Video (Coming Soon)
- Intelligent Content Enhancements

Every capability is designed around one objective:

**Help software teams communicate products with clarity, consistency, and speed.**
