AI Studio is DemoKraft AI's content creation layer. Bring in a screen recording, a presentation deck, or capture your screen live right in your browser — and AI Studio transforms it into a polished MP4 video, a shareable step-by-step doc, and a looping GIF, all in one pass. No video editing software, no creative agency, and no re-recording needed: every team member from sales engineer to HR business partner can produce broadcast-quality content in minutes.
