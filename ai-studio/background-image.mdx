## Welcome to the Walkthrough

In this guide will show how to apply background image to your videos.

## Opening Your Project and Accessing Background Settings

Begin by opening your project in editor mode. From there, navigate directly to the Background section where you can manage and customize your video's background image.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184149-1024x540.png" alt="Background Settings Panel" />
</Frame>

## Activating Background Effects

To apply a background effect, simply toggle the activation button. This enables background customization options, allowing you to enhance your video's visual appeal effortlessly.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184218-1024x536.png" alt="Toggle Background Activation" />
</Frame>

## Selecting a Background Image

Choose a background image that aligns with your brand's identity. Selecting the right image helps reinforce your brand message and creates a cohesive viewing experience.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184555-1024x540.png" alt="Selecting Background Image Template" />
</Frame>

## Adjusting Screen Ratio and Visual Effects

Fine-tune the screen ratio to fit your video's layout, round the corners for a polished look, and adjust the shadow opacity. These settings enhance your video effects and contribute to a professional presentation.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184733-1024x538.png" alt="Screen Ratio and Layout Adjustments" />
</Frame>

## Customizing Shadow Intensity and Corner Sharpness

You can modify the shadow intensity to add depth, sharpen the video corners for clarity, and further adjust the screen ratio. These options provide you with precise control to optimize the visual quality of your presentation.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184912-1024x536.png" alt="Shadow and Corner Fine-Tuning" />
</Frame>

## Saving Your Background Customizations

After making your adjustments, click the Apply button to save your changes. If you dont click apply your changes will be discarded. This ensures that all your customizations are preserved and ready for use in your video project.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-184932-1024x536.png" alt="Clicking the Apply Button" />
</Frame>

## Viewing the Final Background Effect

Your background image combined with the applied effects will be displayed in realtime in canvas. You can tweak and adjust config in the config panel as needed and click apply to save the changes.
To remove an applied music, disable the toggle switch at the top and click apply.

  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-185003-1024x543.png" alt="Final Render Preview" />
</Frame>
