Getting content into AI Studio takes less than a minute regardless of what you're starting from. Upload an existing screen recording from any tool you already use, import a PowerPoint or Google Slides deck and let AI generate the narration from your speaker notes, or capture a live product walkthrough straight from your browser without leaving the platform. Whichever path you choose, AI Studio handles transcription, script enhancement, and timeline preparation automatically so you can jump straight to reviewing and refining.

## Three Ways to Start a Project

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upload a Recording">
    Use this option if you already have a screen capture from Loom, OBS, QuickTime, Camtasia, or any other recorder.

    **Supported formats:** `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.webm`

    Drag your file onto the **New Project** screen, or click **Browse** to select it from your file system. Set a project name and click **Create** to begin processing.

    ### File Size and Duration Limits

    | Plan | Max duration | Max file size |
    |---|---|---|
    | Free Trial | 10 min | ~500 MB |
    | Starter | 30 min | ~1.5 GB |
    | Growth | 150 min | ~7.5 GB |
    | Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

    <Tip>
      Record at 1080p or higher at 30 or 60 fps, and use a close microphone for the best AI transcription quality. The AI handles filler words and background noise, but a clean input always produces sharper results.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import a Deck">
    Use this option if your content exists as a PowerPoint or Google Slides presentation. AI Studio reads each slide, extracts your speaker notes (or the slide text if no notes are present), and generates a fully narrated walkthrough video automatically.

    **Supported formats:** `.pptx`, Google Slides exported as `.pptx`

    1. Click **New Project** → **Upload a Deck**.
    2. Upload your `.pptx` file.
    3. Review the auto-generated, slide-by-slide script in the Script panel.
    4. Approve the script to trigger voiceover generation.

    <Tip>
      Add speaker notes to each slide before exporting. AI Studio uses these as the primary narration source and produces much richer output than from slide titles and bullet points alone.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Record in Browser">
    Use this option to capture a live product walkthrough without leaving the platform.

    1. Click **New Project** → **Record Now**.
    2. Grant screen capture permission when your browser prompts you.
    3. Choose your recording source: **Full Screen**, a specific **App Window** (recommended — isolates your product from other apps), or a **Browser Tab**.
    4. Optionally enable your **microphone** to narrate as you record.
    5. Click **Start Recording** and walk through your product flows.
    6. Click **Stop Recording** when done — the file saves automatically as a new project.

    <Tip>
      You don't need to narrate perfectly. AI Script Enhancement refines your narration and AI Voiceover replaces the audio track entirely. Focus on showing the right product flows — the AI handles everything else.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## After Upload: What Happens Next?

Once your file is uploaded or your recording stops, AI Studio automatically:

1. **Transcribes** your audio — or extracts text from slide speaker notes.
2. **Analyses** the content structure and identifies key product moments.
3. **Generates** an enhanced script suggestion mapped to the video timeline.
4. **Prepares** the timeline for visual effect placement.

You receive an in-app notification and an email when your project is ready to edit. Processing typically completes within 5 minutes for files under 30 minutes.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upload fails or times out">
    Confirm your file is in `.mp4`, `.mov`, or `.webm` format and that the file size is within your plan's limit. Try a different browser or clear your browser cache, then re-upload.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Format not supported error">
    Convert your file to MP4 before uploading. HandBrake (free, cross-platform) is the fastest option — select the H.264 MP4 preset and export.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Processing is slow">
    Files over 1 GB can take 10–15 minutes to process. You will receive an email notification as soon as processing completes — no need to keep the tab open.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Poor audio quality in the transcript">
    Re-record using a headset or an external USB microphone, and record in a quiet environment. The AI transcription engine is sensitive to background noise, especially keyboard sounds and room echo.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
