Every time you upload a recording or import a deck, AI Script Enhancement runs automatically on your narration. It strips filler words, shortens sentences to a natural spoken cadence, reframes feature statements as benefit statements, and adds transitions that carry your viewer from one idea to the next — all without changing your intent or the key points you made. You review and approve every line before a single word goes to voiceover. Nothing reaches your video without your explicit sign-off.

## How It Works

**Raw audio → Transcription → AI Enhancement → Script panel**

1. **Transcription:** AI Studio transcribes your original audio into raw text, filler words and all. Click **View Original** in the Script panel at any time to see exactly what you said, word for word.

2. **Enhancement:** The AI rewrites the raw transcript using these principles:
   - Remove filler words (_"um"_, _"uh"_, _"you know"_, _"basically"_)
   - Shorten sentences to a natural spoken cadence of 10–15 words
   - Reframe feature statements as benefit statements — _"this button saves your settings"_ becomes _"your settings save automatically — no extra steps"_
   - Add logical transitions between sections so the narrative flows
   - Close with a clear next step or call to action

3. **Script panel:** The enhanced script appears on the right side of the editor. Each segment maps to a time range in the timeline — click any segment to jump to that exact moment in the video.

## Review and Approve Your Script

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Script panel">
    After processing completes, open your project. The Script panel loads automatically on the right side of the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review each segment">
    Read through the enhanced script. Click any segment to jump to the corresponding moment in the video preview and confirm the narration matches what's on screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit inline if needed">
    Click directly into any segment to edit the text. Changes save automatically. Keep sentences to 10–15 words and lead with the benefit, not the feature name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the tone">
    Use the **Tone** selector above the Script panel to choose the register that fits your audience:

    | Tone | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Professional** (default) | B2B sales demos, product walkthroughs |
    | **Educational** | Onboarding videos, customer training |
    | **Concise** | Short-form content, social clips, GIF captions |
    | **Energetic** | Product launch announcements, marketing campaigns |

    Changing the tone and clicking **Re-enhance All** regenerates the full script in the selected register without overwriting your manual edits to individual segments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve the script">
    Click **Approve Script** at the top of the Script panel to lock the script and enable voiceover generation and export.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The script must be in **Approved** state before you can export your video. Unapproved scripts display a yellow indicator in the project status bar.
</Note>

## Editing Tips

- **Keep sentences short.** Spoken sentences land best at 10–15 words. Viewers process audio faster when each sentence carries a single, clear idea.
- **Match narration to the screen.** Each sentence should describe what is happening on screen at that exact moment. If audio and visual drift apart, adjust the script or trim the video segment.
- **Use active voice.** _"The AI qualifies your leads"_ is stronger than _"Your leads are qualified by the AI."_
- **One idea per sentence.** Never combine two separate features or benefits into a single sentence.

## Advanced Editing Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Split a segment">
    Place your cursor at the split point within a segment and press **Ctrl+Enter** (Windows) or **Cmd+Enter** (Mac). The segment divides into two independently editable sections.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Merge segments">
    Select two adjacent segments by clicking the first and Shift-clicking the second, then click **Merge Segments** in the segment toolbar. The two sections combine into one.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Re-enhance a single segment">
    Click a segment to select it, then click the **Re-enhance** button (sparkle icon). The AI regenerates only that segment — all other segments remain unchanged.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lock or unlock a segment">
    Click the **Lock** icon on any segment to protect it from being overwritten by future **Re-enhance All** runs. Click the icon again to unlock it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use **Re-enhance** on individual segments rather than running **Re-enhance All** when you only need to fix one section — it's faster and leaves your approved segments untouched.
</Tip>
