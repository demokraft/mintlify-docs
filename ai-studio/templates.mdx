Templates give your video a professional structure before you record a single second of content. Each template comes with branded motion design, a recommended section layout, and a placeholder script — upload your recording and AI maps your narration to the template's sections automatically. You get launch-quality output from the very first pass, and every element remains fully editable so you can tailor the result to your exact needs.

## Template Categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Product Demo">
    **Ideal length:** 3–7 minutes

    Structured for a complete B2B product walkthrough with a narrative arc from problem to proof.

    | Section | Duration |
    |---|---|
    | Intro scene (branded) | 3 sec |
    | Problem statement | ~30 sec |
    | Product overview | ~60 sec |
    | Feature walkthrough 1 | 60–90 sec |
    | Feature walkthrough 2 | 60–90 sec |
    | Outcome / ROI summary | ~30 sec |
    | Outro with CTA | 5 sec |

    **Best for:** Sales demo videos, prospect outreach, website hero videos.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Product Announcement">
    **Ideal length:** 60–90 seconds

    A tight, energetic format built for feature launch communications.

    | Section | Duration |
    |---|---|
    | Intro with feature name | 3 sec |
    | What's new | ~30 sec |
    | How to use it | 30–45 sec |
    | Outro | 5 sec |

    **Best for:** Feature release notes, product update emails, social media announcements, changelog entries.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Onboarding Sequence">
    **Ideal length:** 2–4 minutes per video

    A series format with chapter markers and consistent visual styling across all videos in the sequence — designed to be grouped into a playlist in AI Hub.

    **Includes:** Chapter markers, persistent branding across all episodes, and a consistent Intro/Outro Motion Scene style so every video in the series feels like one cohesive product.

    **Best for:** Customer onboarding playlists in AI Hub, in-app help content, email onboarding sequences.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Sales Follow-Up">
    **Ideal length:** 60–90 seconds

    A compact, personalised format with a prominent CTA — built to be recorded quickly and sent immediately after a demo call.

    **Best for:** Post-demo follow-up emails, outbound sales prospecting, LinkedIn video messages.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Training Module">
    **Ideal length:** 5–15 minutes

    A structured instructional format with clearly defined learning phases.

    | Section | Purpose |
    |---|---|
    | Learning objectives | Set expectations upfront |
    | Step-by-step walkthrough | Core instructional content |
    | Knowledge check slide | Reinforce key takeaways |

    **Best for:** Employee onboarding, compliance training, product certification content, L&D programmes.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Use a Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the template library">
    Click **New Project** → **Start from Template** from the AI Studio dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template">
    Browse the template gallery and click any template to preview its section structure and Motion Scene style. Click **Use This Template** to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your content">
    Upload an existing recording, import a presentation deck, or record directly in the browser — whichever method suits your source material.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let AI map your content">
    AI Studio analyses your narration and maps it to the template's section structure. Sections that run shorter or longer than the template's recommended duration are flagged for your review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit">
    Open the project and refine it. Every element is fully editable: Motion Scenes (text, colours, style), section order (drag in the timeline), and the script (edit inline in the Script panel as usual).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom Templates

If you build a structure you want to reuse, save it as a workspace template:

1. Open the **Timeline** panel in any completed project.
2. Click **Save as Template**.
3. Give it a name (e.g. _"APAC Sales Demo 2026"_).
4. The template appears in your workspace's template gallery under **Custom Templates**, accessible to all team members.

<Info>
  Custom template saving is available on **Growth** and **Enterprise** plans. Enterprise workspaces can also request custom templates created and maintained by the DemoKraft AI team.
</Info>
