## Preparing Your Presentation for Upload

To upload your presentation, click on **Upload Presentation**. 

<Note>
  **File Requirements:**
  * Must be in **PDF format** (each slide on a separate page).
  * Maximum length of **15 slides**.
  * *Note:* Currently, only PDF files are supported, but support for PowerPoint files is coming soon.
</Note>


---

## Upload and Preprocessing Workflow

Once you upload your file, it starts preprocessing and it opens directly within our editor.  

While this happens, our system performs essential internal preprocessing, similar to our video upload process. Please allow a moment for this to complete.
