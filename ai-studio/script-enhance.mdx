## Achieving Studio-Quality Scripts and Brand-Aligned Narration

In this section, we show you how to improve your script to a studio-standard level and produce narration that aligns perfectly with your brand's voice, ensuring consistency and professionalism throughout your video.

## Opening Your Project in the Editor Workspace

Once you have an existing project or a completed recording, it opens directly in the editor – your workspace where you can prepare, refine, and apply voice-over elements seamlessly to your video content.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-175334-1024x512.png" alt="Editor Workspace" />

## Accessing Script and Voice-Over Enhancement Tools

After your project loads in editor mode, navigate to the left menu and select the Script option. This brings you to the panel where you can enhance your script and voice-over settings effectively. 

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181004-1024x492.png" alt="Script Menu Option" />
</Frame>

## Enhancing Your Video’s Script: Two Options

Within the Script panel, firstly if you want, you can review manually and make the edits you need. Next step is to enhance your video's script. You have two main options to choose from to suit your workflow and goals.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181027-1024x511.png" alt="Script Enhancement Options" />
</Frame>

## Using AI Rewrite for Full Script Enhancement

Utilize the AI Rewrite button at the top to enhance your entire script in one single step. This method is ideal for fast, consistent improvements across your whole project, saving you time while boosting quality.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181046-1024x509.png" alt="AI Rewrite Tool" />
</Frame>

## Enhancing Individual Script Segments

Alternatively, select a specific segment to rewrite. This targeted approach lets you enhance particular lines without altering the rest of your script, giving you precise control where needed.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181550-1024x512.png" alt="Individual Segment Rewrite" />

## Benefits of Script Enhancement and Voice Selection

Whether you enhance your script fully or by segment, this process elevates your script to studio quality and eliminates common issues. Next, choose from the available voices to preview and apply to your narration.

## Choosing and Applying the Best Voice for Your Brand

Explore the voice options and select the one that best fits your brand identity. Once chosen, apply the selected voice to your project to unify the narration style and enhance audience connection.

  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_7-1024x661.jpg" alt="Applying Brand Voice" />

## Enabling Speech Generation

After applying a voice, the Generate Speech button becomes active, allowing you to proceed with producing the voice-over narration based on your script and voice choice.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181646-1024x509.png" alt="Active Generate Speech Button" />
</Frame>

## Generating Voice-Over for Your Script

Click Generate Speech to start creating the voice-over for your entire script. This initiates a background process that applies narration to your video, automating voice production while maintaining quality. Visuals will be auto-synched to the voiceover which can change the duration of your video. You can now play the video in player to check the new voiceover and iterate as needed.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181707-1024x513.png" alt="Voiceover Generation Progress" />
</Frame>

## Processing Time Based on Video Length

Keep in mind that processing time varies depending on your video's length. Longer videos require more time for narration generation, so please be patient as the system completes this step.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_10-1-1024x661.jpg" alt="Processing Status" />

## Regenerating Speech After Script Edits

If you edit your script in a single segment or enhance the full script, remember to regenerate the speech. This ensures the audio narration matches your most current script version accurately.

<Frame>
  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/Screenshot-2025-12-11-181742-1024x511.png" alt="Regenerate Speech UI" />
</Frame>

## Background Regeneration of Segment Narration

By clicking Generate Speech again, you trigger a background regeneration of narration for the selected segment, using the voice you previously selected to maintain brand consistency.

  <img src="https://demokraft.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/frame_12-1-1024x661.jpg" alt="Segment Narration Updates" />

## Aligning Voice-Over with Your Revised Script
