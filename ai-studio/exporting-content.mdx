Every completed DemoKraft AI project generates three ready-to-use assets from a single **Export** action: a polished MP4 video, a hosted step-by-step doc with annotated screenshots, and a looping GIF. You don't need to do anything extra to get all three — they are all derived from your approved script and voiceover automatically, so the moment your video is done, your doc and GIF are done too.

## Export Formats

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Video (MP4)">
    Click **Export** → **Video (MP4)** and configure your export settings:

    | Setting | Options |
    |---|---|
    | **Resolution** | 720p (Free Trial), 2K (Starter), 4K (Growth / Enterprise) |
    | **Frame rate** | 24 fps, 30 fps, or 60 fps |
    | **Compression** | Standard (smaller file) or High Quality (larger file) |
    | **Subtitles** | None, embedded in video, or separate SRT file |
    | **Language version** | Choose which language to export if you have translations |

    Click **Export Video**. Export time is typically 2–10 minutes depending on resolution and video length. You receive an email notification when the file is ready to download from the **Exports** panel.

    <Warning>
      Free Trial exports include a DemoKraft AI watermark in the lower-right corner. Upgrade to any paid plan to remove it.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Step-by-Step Doc">
    Click **Export** → **Step-by-Step Doc** and choose your delivery format:

    | Format | Best for |
    |---|---|
    | **Hosted web link** | Help centres, product portals, CRM follow-up emails, in-app contextual help |
    | **PDF download** | Sales proposals, event handouts, customer onboarding packs |

    The hosted web link is published at `docs.demokraft.ai/[your-workspace]/[project-id]` by default, or on your custom domain on Growth and Enterprise plans. The web version is searchable, mobile-responsive, and embeddable in any webpage with a single `<iframe>` snippet.

    Copy the public URL to share the doc directly, or click **Download PDF** to save it locally.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GIF">
    Click **Export** → **GIF** and select your clip:

    1. Drag the **start** and **end** handles on the timeline to define the section to export (maximum 30 seconds).
    2. Choose a quality setting:
       - **Standard** — optimised file size for email and web
       - **High** — sharper quality for presentations and pitch decks
    3. Click **Export GIF** and download.

    **Typical uses:**
    - Email signature showing a key product feature in motion
    - Slack or Teams message teasing a new feature to a prospect
    - LinkedIn or X (Twitter) social post
    - Product changelog entry
    - Embedded in a sales proposal or pitch deck
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Send Directly to AI Hub

Skip the download-and-re-upload cycle entirely. Click **Send to Hub** in the Export panel to push your video directly to your AI Hub content library. From there, add it to a playlist or configure an AI Demo Agent session — no file management required.

## Managing Your Exports

Find all export jobs under the **Exports** tab in any project. From there you can:

- Download previously exported files
- Re-export with different settings at any time (resolution, subtitles, language)
- View export history and file sizes
- Delete old exports to free up storage

<Note>
  Exports are retained for **90 days** from the export date. After 90 days, the exported files are removed from storage — but your project source is always retained and you can re-export at any time.
</Note>
