### Navigating to Billing from Settings

To open your billing management interface:
1. Locate the **Settings** menu on the left-hand navigation pane.
2. Select the **Billing** option. 

This provides step-by-step access to each billing feature and option available in Demokraft AI.

## Managing Your Subscriptions

### Hub Subscription Details

When monitoring your **Hub** subscription layout, you can inspect and modify several operational parameters:

* **Credits Tracking:** Review total session credits consumed.
* **Plan Status:** Check active status and current renewal cycles.
* **Playlist Usage:** Monitor the total number of active playlists.
* **Billing Interval:** Determine if your plan structure is set to a monthly or yearly cycle.

<Note>
  You are free to switch plan tiers, update, or cancel your active subscription at any time to maintain fine-grained control over your subscription.
</Note>

### Studio Subscription Usage Limits

For **Studio** subscriptions, specialized production metrics are provided to keep track of your creation capacity:

1. **Active Tier:** View your current studio subscription plan details.
2. **Video Output:** Monitor the total number of videos created.
3. **Export Counts:** Track raw file exports processed.
4. **AI Voiceover Consumption:** Check your AI voiceover usage parameters.
5. **Project Constraints:** Monitor allocated project limits.

<Tip>
  **Reaching Project Limits:** Upon hitting your maximum project count allocation, you can remove or archive older projects to instantly free up creation capacity for new production pipelines.
</Tip>

### Reviewing Studio Billing Parameters

The Studio overview page provides quick visibility into your subscription details, helping you stay ahead of renewals and plan alterations:
* **Current Billing Period:** Track exact cycle timelines.
* **Active Plan Information:** View current feature permissions.
* **Upcoming Plan Details:** Verify scheduled updates or queue changes.
* **Renewal Cycle Schedule:** Anticipate payment schedules proactively.
