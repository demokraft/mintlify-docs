## Common Issues

### Password Expiry or Mismatch
If your password has expired, or if you are seeing an incorrect password error during login:
* Go to the main login page.
* Click on the **Forgot Password** link.
* Enter your registered email address to receive a secure password reset link.

### Slow Processing or Loading Times
Some operations—such as video uploads, AI voice generation, processing presentation slides, or document generation—may take a few minutes to complete. 
* **Processing speeds depend directly on the duration of your video or the specific features being used.**
* Please be patient and avoid refreshing or closing the browser tab while an active operation is running.
