DemoKraft AI is available across four AI Studio plan tiers — from a 14-day Free Trial to a fully custom Enterprise plan — giving your team exactly the capacity and features you need. Layer on the optional AI Hub add-on to enable AI Demo Agent sessions, or contact sales for a bespoke AI Creative project. All paid plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time, with the option to switch to annual billing and lock in a 20% discount.

## AI Hub Add-On

AI Hub is an optional add-on you can layer on top of any paid AI Studio plan. It unlocks AI Demo Agent Guided Sessions, priced by monthly session volume. A session is counted each time a unique visitor starts an AI Demo Agent Guided Session — Static Playlist and Conversational Playlist views are not counted.

| AI Hub Plan | Price | Sessions included | Overage rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hub Starter** | $50/month | 50 sessions | $1.50/session |
| **Hub Growth** | $150/month | 150 sessions | $1.00/session |
| **Hub Enterprise** | Custom | Custom | Custom |

<Info>
  Unused sessions do not roll over to the next month. If you consistently reach your session limit, upgrade to the next Hub tier to lower your per-session overage rate.
</Info>

## Annual Billing

Switch to annual billing at any time to save 20% compared to the monthly rate. When you switch, your remaining monthly balance is credited toward your annual plan — you are never charged twice.

To switch, go to **Settings → Billing → Switch to Annual** and confirm your selection.

| Plan | Monthly (billed monthly) | Monthly (billed annually) | Annual total |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $30/month | $24/month | $288/year |
| **Growth** | $150/month | $120/month | $1,440/year |
| **Hub Starter** | $50/month | $40/month | $480/year |
| **Hub Growth** | $150/month | $120/month | $1,440/year |

<Tip>
  Annual billing locks in your current rate and saves 20% across both your AI Studio plan and any AI Hub add-on. Switch at any time from **Settings → Billing → Switch to Annual**.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When do upgrades and downgrades take effect?">
    Upgrades apply immediately — your new plan limits are available as soon as you confirm, and you are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, so you retain your existing plan's features until the cycle closes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when I exceed my project limit?">
    You receive an email notification when you reach 80% of your monthly project limit. If you hit the limit before the cycle resets, you can purchase a one-time project top-up from **Settings → Billing** to keep creating without waiting for the next cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do AI Hub session credits roll over?">
    No. Unused sessions expire at the end of each monthly billing cycle and do not carry forward. If you regularly exceed your allowance, upgrade to the next Hub tier to access a lower overage rate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my Hub session limit?">
    Sessions above your monthly allowance are billed as overages: $1.50 per additional session on Hub Starter and $1.00 per additional session on Hub Growth. You will receive an email alert when you reach 80% of your session limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do seats count individual users or concurrent users?">
    Seats are per individual user account. Each person on your team who needs to create or edit content requires their own seat. Viewer access is always unlimited and free on every plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
