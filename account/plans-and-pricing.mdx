DemoKraft AI is available across four AI Studio plan tiers — from a 14-day Free Trial to a fully custom Enterprise plan — giving your team exactly the capacity and features you need. Layer on the optional AI Hub add-on to enable AI Agent sessions, or contact sales for a bespoke AI Creative project. All paid plans are billed monthly and can be cancelled at any time, with the option to switch to annual billing and lock in a 20% discount.

You have the flexibility to choose Studio only, Hub only or Studio+Hub subscriptions as per your needs.

For AI Studio and Hub plan details, refer to the [Plans and Pricing page](https://demokraft.ai/plans-and-pricing/).

## AI Studio Plans

AI Studio helps teams turn raw recordings, videos, or presentations into polished, brand-ready product content using AI-assisted scripts, voiceovers, and visuals.

### Studio Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are Studio video projects?">
    In Studio, every video you record or upload, or presentation you upload, becomes available as a project in AI Studio. When you translate a project from one language to another, a new project is created for the target language. You can see the project list under the Studio section in the app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I see my current usage and limits?">
    Under the **Settings** section in the app, there is a **Billing** subsection. You can go to the "Studio Overview" to see your plan details like subscribed usage and available limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the New video projects limit?">
    This is the number of new projects you can create every month. This limit is reset at the end of your billing cycle. Please note that during translation, a new project is created and it will consume from this new video project limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do Video export minutes in a plan work?">
    Each Studio plan has a specific duration up to which you can export a video. Let's say you are on the Free Studio plan and it allows up to 10 mins of export video duration; you can export videos until the total sum of all the exported videos reaches that month's export duration limit. 
    
    If the duration of the video you are trying to export exceeds the available limit, it won't allow you to export. You will need to upgrade to a higher plan if you need more export video duration. The same export video duration applies to both video and GIF exports. For example: if you have a 2-minute video to be exported, exporting both the video and the GIF for that video will consume 2+2 (4) minutes from your plan's export duration limit. This limit resets at the end of every billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the Max video projects limit in a plan?">
    This is the total maximum limit on the number of Studio projects you can have at any given time. If your plan allows a max of 3 projects, you can have up to 3 projects. This limit does not reset every month. If you reach the max limit, you will need to delete an existing project to make way for new projects. If you need a higher limit, upgrade to a higher plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does the Brand kit include?">
    The Brand kit allows for custom aspects like custom voices (coming soon), uploading custom background images (coming soon), uploading custom background music, premium voiceover capabilities, and more.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are seats?">
    Seats allow multiple users to collaborate using a single DemoKraft account so that multiple users can create content, review, and collaborate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Translation?">
    Once you record or upload your video in one language, you can translate it into 39 different languages to make your content multi-lingual and reach a global audience. You can refer to the General Studio FAQ for details on the languages supported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why are there multiple subscriptions?">
    To provide flexibility to users, we have kept separate subscriptions for Studio and Hub. These are independent of each other. You can control your subscription plans from the **Billing** subsection in the **Settings** section of the app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I choose different plans?">
    When you sign up, your account defaults to the free plan for both Hub and Studio. You can check the plan details for your account by going to the **Billing** subsection in **Settings**. If you want to change the plan for either Studio or Hub, you can do so from there.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='What does "Number of AI Voice Characters" mean?'>
    The number of AI Voice characters refers to how much AI-generated voice you can create based on your subscription plan. 
    
    As a general guideline, about 1,000 characters of script equals approximately 1 minute of AI-generated voice. The total AI voice duration used depends on the number of characters in your video script. If you generate an AI voice for your video, the characters in your script will be counted against your plan’s AI Voice character limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are payments supported for Indian cards?">
    Due to RBI guidelines in India, automatic renewals require user mandates and authentication. Because of this, subscription renewals and upgrades are handled through manual payment links.

    **For renewals or upgrades:**
    - You will receive an invoice with a payment link.
    - Use the payment link to complete your payment before the due date.
    - If payment is not completed on time, your subscription will be:
      - Downgraded to the Free plan (for Hub)
      - Deactivated (for Studio)
    
    During your active subscription, Indian cards will work normally.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I update my billing information?">
    You can update your billing details—such as name and address—from the Billing Form tab.
    
    **To update your billing information:**
    1. Go to **Account Settings**
    2. Open the **Billing** section
    3. Update your details in the **Billing Form** tab
    
    *Note: Billing details must be updated in the Billing Form to be reflected correctly on your invoices.*
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI Hub

AI Hub delivers instant engagement through playlists. Conversations and AI-guided journeys help buyers and viewers explore without waiting for scheduled manual demos, training, or engagement sessions.

### Hub Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I want to switch between pricing tiers?">
    We understand that your business needs can evolve, and DemoKraft AI is designed with flexibility in mind. You have the freedom to switch between our pricing tiers to find the best fit for your current requirements.

    - **How to Switch:** You can easily upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time directly through your DemoKraft AI account dashboard. The process is designed to be straightforward.
    - **Billing Adjustments:** When you change tiers, any adjustments to your billing are handled fairly and are typically prorated. 
      - *Upgrades:* If you upgrade, you’ll generally be charged a prorated amount for the difference in tier cost for the remainder of your current billing cycle. Your subsequent billing will reflect the new, higher-tier price.
      - *Downgrades:* If you downgrade, the change will usually take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
    - **Customized Billing Discussions:** We recognize that some organizations may have unique billing requirements. We are open to discussing customized billing arrangements, especially for larger accounts or specific contract terms, to best accommodate your needs. Please feel free to reach out to our sales or support teams to explore these options.
    
    Our goal is to provide a seamless and transparent experience, ensuring your DemoKraft AI plan always aligns with your business objectives.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Demo Session Credit?">
    Think of Demo Session Credits as your key to unlocking interactive demo experiences for your website visitors. Here’s how they work:
    
    1. Each DemoKraft AI billing plan includes a specific number of Demo Session Credits.
    2. When a visitor on your website engages with and views a DemoKraft AI-powered demo, one Demo Session Credit is utilized from your plan’s allowance.
    3. It’s straightforward: each unique demo viewing session by a visitor consumes one credit. For instance, if a single visitor initiates and views a demo twice (at different times or by re-engaging the widget for a new session), this would count as two Demo Session Credits used. These credits ensure you have a clear and predictable way to manage access to your dynamic demo content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title='What is a "Seat" in DemoKraft AI?'>
    A “Seat” in DemoKraft AI refers to an individual user license that grants a member of your team access to your organization’s DemoKraft AI account. Here’s why seats are important:
    
    1. **Team Collaboration:** With multiple seats, different members of your organization (such as sales representatives, marketing specialists, or demo creators) can log in to collaborate on creating, managing, distributing, and analyzing your interactive demos.
    2. **Centralized Management:** Your organization’s DemoKraft AI account acts as a central hub.
    3. **Admin Control:** The primary account administrator can easily add new users (assigning them a seat), define their roles, and manage permissions. This ensures secure and organized access, allowing you to control who can do what within the platform. 
    
    Essentially, each seat empowers another team member to leverage the full potential of DemoKraft AI.
  </Accordion>

## Annual Billing

Switch to annual billing at any time to save 20% compared to the monthly rate. When you switch, your remaining monthly balance is credited toward your annual plan — you are never charged twice.

To switch, go to **Settings → Billing → Switch to Annual** and confirm your selection.

<Tip>
  Annual billing locks in your current rate and saves 20% across both your AI Studio plan and any AI Hub add-on. Switch at any time from **Settings → Billing → Switch to Annual**.
</Tip>
