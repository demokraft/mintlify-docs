**Helping Software Teams Create Product Content that Drives Growth.**

## Problem being solved

Modern software is becoming more powerful every day.

Yet explaining software has never been harder.

Companies invest heavily in building great products, attracting visitors, and generating leads—but many potential customers leave before fully understanding what the product does, why it matters, or how it solves their problems.

Today's buyers expect instant understanding through engaging product experiences—not lengthy documentation or scheduled demonstrations.

At the same time, every team inside a software company depends on compelling product content.

As products evolve rapidly, creating and maintaining high-quality product content has become one of the biggest operational challenges for software organizations.

## **Introducing Demokraft AI**

### **The Product Content Platform**

Demokraft AI helps software teams create **studio-quality product content** faster through an intuitive visual editor purpose-built for software products.

Whether starting from a screen recording, creating graphical product videos with motion graphics, customizing professional templates, or soon generating complete product videos from a simple prompt, teams can create polished, brand-consistent product experiences in minutes instead of days.

Built-in intelligent capabilities simplify content creation, multilingual communication, and localization, including support for **screen recording in 14 languages** and **translation into 39 languages**.

## **One Platform. Three Powerful Solutions.**

### **🎬 Demokraft AI Studio — You Create**

Create professional product videos, guides, GIFs, and graphical product stories through a modern visual editor with templates, reusable objects, motion graphics, branding, AI voiceovers, and localization.

**Coming Soon:** Generate complete product videos from a simple prompt, then refine them visually before publishing.

### **🌐 Demokraft AI Hub — We Host**

Transform product content into an active business asset with interactive demos, secure sharing, playlists, AI-guided experiences, and engagement analytics.

Or simply export and publish your content through your own website, LMS, CMS, knowledge base, sales enablement platform, or any hosting platform of your choice.

### **✨ Demokraft AI Creative — We Create**

For major launches, executive presentations, homepage videos, and premium marketing campaigns, our creative team delivers custom, brand-aligned product storytelling.
