Demokraft AI empowers teams to turn product raw screen recordings or presentations into polished, studio-quality videos, guides and gifs in minutes.

DemoKraft AI is a unified B2B platform that transforms raw screen recordings or presentations into polished product content — then deploys that content as an intelligent, 24/7 lead qualification engine. Whether you're a sales team closing enterprise deals, a customer success team onboarding new users, or an L&D team building training libraries, DemoKraft AI gives every team the power to produce studio-quality video content without a production budget or a film crew.

## The three platform layers

DemoKraft AI is built around three integrated layers that take you from raw recording to revenue impact:

| Layer | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **AI Studio** | Create studio-quality product demo videos, step-by-step docs, and GIFs from any screen recording |
| **AI Hub** | Deploy your content as conversational, AI-guided demo experiences that qualify leads 24/7 |
| **AI Creative** | Commission agency-quality video production for product launches and campaigns |
