The DemoKraft AI Email integration keeps your team informed the moment a high-intent event happens in an AI Hub session. Instead of checking dashboards or waiting for updates, your team receives a structured alert card in the Slack channel of your choice — with the visitor's name, company, qualification score(if applicable), and direct links to their Lead/Viewer Intelligence profile.

## What it sends

The Email bot posts an alert when any of the following events occur:

- A session completion score (if applicable).
- A visitor event like CTA button clicked.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Email">
    Go to **Integrations → Email** and click toggle switch and click configure icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Email ids you want to send alerts on.">
    In the Email integration settings, add email ids to send alerts on. Click **Save**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your alert triggers">
    Click **Settings -> Notifications** and set your preferences in Notifications config.

  </Step>
</Steps>
