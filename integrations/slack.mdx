The DemoKraft AI Slack integration keeps your team informed the moment a high-intent event happens in an AI Hub session. Instead of checking dashboards or waiting for updates, your team receives a structured alert card in the Slack channel of your choice — with the visitor's name, company, qualification score(if applicable), and direct links to their Lead/Viewer Intelligence profile.

## What it sends

The Slack bot posts an alert when any of the following events occur:

- A session completion score (if applicable).
- A visitor event like a CTA button clicked.

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Slack">
    Go to **Integrations → Slack** and click **Connect**. Complete the OAuth flow and select the Slack workspace you want to connect. Approve Demokraft AI Slack app access. You are returned to DemoKraft AI once the connection is authorised.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your notification channel">
    In the Slack integration settings, click **Select Channel** and choose the channel where alerts should be posted — for example, `#sales-alerts` or `#Training-Users`. Click **Save**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your alert triggers">
    Click **Settings -> Notifications** and set your preferences in Notifications config.

  </Step>
</Steps>
