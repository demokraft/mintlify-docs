DemoKraft AI connects directly to the tools your teams already rely on. Every time a visitor completes an Hub session, user intelligence flows automatically into your tools.

## Native integrations

These integrations are built directly into DemoKraft AI and configured in **Settings → Integrations**.

| Integration | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Slack** | Sends real-time alerts for hot leads/viewers, session completions, and CTA clicked |
| **Email** | Sends real-time emails for hot leads/viewers, session completions, and CTA clicked |
| **HubSpot** (Coming Soon) | Syncs lead profiles, qualification scores, and session data to contacts and timeline events |
| **Salesforce** (Coming Soon) | Pushes lead profiles and scores to Leads, Contacts, and Activities |
| **Calendly** (Coming Soon) | Adds a meeting booking CTA inside sessions; bookings sync to your CRM |
| **Zapier** (Coming Soon) | Triggers workflows in 5,000+ apps when a Hub session completes |

## How to connect any integration

Follow these steps to activate any integration in DemoKraft AI:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** from your DemoKraft AI dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the integration">
    Locate the integration you want to connect — use the search bar or browse by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Click **Connect** and complete the authentication flow. Slack use OAuth;
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set your sync preferences — contact matching rules, notification channels, trigger conditions, or field mappings — depending on the integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The integration is now active and will process data from your next completed session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How data flows after a session

When a visitor completes an AI Hub session, DemoKraft AI processes and routes data in this sequence:

1. **Session ends** — the AI Hub session concludes and the visitor's engagement is recorded.
2. **Lead/Visitor profile created** — DemoKraft AI generates a Lead/Visitor Intelligence profile with qualification score (if applicable), content watched, and questions asked.
3. **Slack alert fires** — a real-time alert is posted to your chosen Slack channel.
4. Support for more tool integrautions is coming soon. Support for custom tool can be planned as part of Enterprise plan.
