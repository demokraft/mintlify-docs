DemoKraft AI connects directly to the tools your sales and marketing teams already rely on. Every time a visitor completes an AI Hub session, lead intelligence flows automatically into your CRM, calendar, and communication tools — no manual data entry, no copy-paste, no dropped signals. Whether you use HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Calendly, or any of thousands of apps via Zapier, your pipeline stays current the moment a session ends.

## Native integrations

These integrations are built directly into DemoKraft AI and configured in **Settings → Integrations**.

| Integration | What it does |
|---|---|
| **HubSpot** | Syncs lead profiles, qualification scores, and session data to contacts and timeline events |
| **Salesforce** | Pushes lead profiles and scores to Leads, Contacts, and Activities |
| **Slack** | Sends real-time alerts for hot leads, session completions, and meeting bookings |
| **Calendly** | Adds a meeting booking CTA inside sessions; bookings sync to your CRM |
| **Zapier** | Triggers workflows in 5,000+ apps when a Hub session completes |

## How to connect any integration

Follow these steps to activate any integration in DemoKraft AI:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations** from your DemoKraft AI dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the integration">
    Locate the integration you want to connect — use the search bar or browse by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Click **Connect** and complete the authentication flow. HubSpot, Salesforce, and Slack use OAuth; Zapier and Calendly use an API key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Set your sync preferences — contact matching rules, notification channels, trigger conditions, or field mappings — depending on the integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The integration is now active and will process data from your next completed session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How data flows after a session

When a visitor completes an AI Hub session, DemoKraft AI processes and routes data in this sequence:

1. **Session ends** — the AI Hub session concludes and the visitor's engagement is recorded.
2. **Lead profile created** — DemoKraft AI generates a Lead Intelligence profile with qualification score, content watched, and questions asked.
3. **CRM record created or updated** — the profile is pushed to HubSpot or Salesforce as a new or updated contact, with a timeline event or activity logged.
4. **Slack alert fires** — if the qualification score exceeds your configured threshold, a real-time alert is posted to your chosen Slack channel.
5. **Calendly booking syncs** — if a meeting was booked during the session, it appears on the contact record in your CRM and in your Calendly calendar.
6. **Zapier triggers fire** — any active Zaps listening to the matching trigger event run their configured actions in connected apps.

## Custom integrations via Webhooks

For any tool not covered by a native integration or Zapier, configure a Webhook to receive session data directly. DemoKraft AI sends a structured JSON payload to your endpoint whenever a selected event occurs.

**To set up a Webhook:**

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Webhooks**.
2. Click **Add Webhook**.
3. Enter your endpoint URL.
4. Select the events you want to receive.
5. Click **Save**. DemoKraft AI will begin sending payloads to your endpoint immediately.

**Configurable events:**

- Session Completed
- Hot Lead
- Meeting Booked
- Trial Started

The full JSON schema is available in **Settings → Integrations → Webhooks → View Schema**.

<Note>
  Webhooks are available on all plans. The full JSON payload includes the complete Lead Intelligence profile for the session.
</Note>
